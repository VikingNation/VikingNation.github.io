Ham radio operating events recognize important places, people, and times. Check out the 2026 World Soccer Tournament special event. Look for stations calling CQ WC, have the WC prefix, or are on the list of stations activating. Get at least one station and you qualify for a certificate. Get more stations to progress through the levels.

Check out the [https://wc2026ses.org](https://wc2026ses.org) website for more information.
