Winlink File Xfer:
a. Find appropriate images, and, if necessary, convert them to JPEG or PNG files. See 'IMAGE CAPTURE PROCEDURES' below.
b. The Winlink attachment Image 'Resizing Tool' will be used. Suggested procedures:  Create 'Message' > 'New Message' > select ?Attachments? > 'Add' your selected image > 'Edit/Resize-Image' > 'Change Size' > make your changes > 'Save? > 'Save Changes' > 'Finished' > Address and post to your Outbox. TASK2: VarAC File Xfer: 
a. Find appropriate images and, if necessary, convert them to JPEG or PNG files. See 'IMAGE CAPTURE PROCEDURES' below.
b. If needed, set up an outgoing files directory in the QSO tab of your Settings, C:\VarAC\Outgoing_Files is suggested.
c. The VarAC 'Image Shrinker' tool will be used. Suggested procedures: go to 'Tools? > Image Shrinker > ?LOAD IMAGE' > resize the image using the 'Resolution' and 'Quality' sliders, if necessary > check the 'My callsign' box > ?SAVE TO DISK?. The file will be saved to your ?Outgoing files directory? by default. 
d. Once connected to your Target Station, use the ?SEND FILE? button, select the file from the Outgoing files directory, or elsewhere. 
e. If relaying via a Target Station, type RELAY TO N3XL in the ?New message? window at the bottom of the main window to inform the Target Station and then SEND.

Fldigi (mode MFSK64) File Xfer: 
a. Review the image transfer training at __

c. Establish a connection with your Target Station, see ESTABLISHING FLDIGI CONNECTIONS below.

* ESTABLISHING FLDIGI CONNECTIONS (P = Participant. TS = Target Station)
P - Tune radio to TS dial frequency. Use QSY button to adjust frequency to the 1500 sweet spot.
TS - QST QST de WX3F WX3F, QST QST de WX3F WX3F K (call periodically, QST=calling all stations).
P - Precisely align cursor to TS signal, to obtain good copy. The display center frequency will normally be at, or near, 1500. 
P - Once good copy of the Target Station is established, respond WX3F WX3F de KB3XYZ KB3XYZ, I HAVE IMAGE FOR RELAY TO N3XL K.
TS - RR KB3XYZ good copy, pls send your picture K.
P ? Send image. Terminate the transmission when image xfer is completed.
TS ? Brief acknowledgement, or request to resend, etc.


## Planning

Plan ahead and use your ingenuity and imagination to find, download, or create multiple images of automobile crashes, wild-fires, hurricane damage, tornado damage, terror attacks, or other natural disasters. A photo from your cell phone photo gallery is a good choice. See the ' IMAGE CAPTURE PROCEDURES' below.

File size limitations to achieve reasonable RF transmission times will vary, depending on the quality of your connection, propagation conditions, and equipment limitations. The nominal file size limit for Winlink and VarAC is 100 Kbytes, which is generally too large for RF transmissions. We are suggesting that you resize your images below 50 Kbytes for Vara FM and 20 Kbytes for HF or Packet. Make adjustments as necessary, if the transmission fails or takes too long.

You are encouraged to send images to as many Target Stations as possible. The HF and VHF Target Station lists will be provided to everyone on the MDC Winlink distribution list on 7 MAY 2024. Carefully observe the modes, frequencies, dates, and times of operation provided.

Winlink Packet P2P and Vara FM P2P users should consider the use of digipeating to extend their range beyond VHF line-of-sight. A VARA license is required to digipeat using Vara FM.

Ensure that your VARA, VarAC, and Winlink software programs are up-to-date.
VARA - https://rosmodem.wordpress.com
VarAC - https://www.varac-hamradio.com
Winlink - https://www.winlink.org/WinlinkExpress
Fldigi - https://sourceforge.net/projects/fldigi/files/
