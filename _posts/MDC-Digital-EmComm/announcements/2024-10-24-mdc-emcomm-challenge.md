## Purpose

In the November Challenge you will simulate participation in an ARES Mutual Assistance Team (ARESMAT) event and provide Health and Welfare (H&W) status messages from a simulated tornado disaster zone. In this Challenge participants will gain familiarity with Radiogram and/or ICS-213 Forms and practice Winlink, VarAC, and Fldigi Peer-to-Peer (P2P) messaging.  Since different ARESMAT teams use differing messaging software, it is important to gain familiarity with as many of them as you can. It is only with trial and error and repeated practice that operator proficiency can be maintained. 

Challenges emphasize P2P messaging because when the internet goes down in a disaster and Ham communications services are called upon, P2P will be the primary transmission method available. HF Radio-only (RO) and Winlink EMCOMM Post Office services may also be available and should be exercised from time to time, as well.
 
Radio Relay International (RRI) Gold (safe and well) texts will be used in Winlink RRI ICS-213 Radiograms, VarAC ICS-213s, and FLMSG Radiograms, in this exercise. ## Task 1

, Winlink messaging: You are simulating an ARESMAT operator deployed to a Tornado disaster zone. Under RADIOGRAM & RRI form templates, select the RRI ICS213 Radiogram template and create a H&W status report message from a person in the disaster zone (see attached Radiogram Instructions). Use the Precedence, Place of Origin, Local time, To: and From: address info provided. Use at least one text selected from Standard RRI GOLD Radiogram texts (see attached). You aren?t limited to the RRI Gold texts, but use at least one of them.  Click on the button to preview for accuracy before submitting. Send your Radiogram to a Winlink P2PTarget Station for relay, or if available, drop it off at the WX3F Winlink Post Office Target Station. The RRI Liaison stations listed as tactical addresses in the drop-down at the bottom of the form are not to be used in the Challenge. You will need to send your message to a Target Station. Put the Target Station callsign in Liaison Station box.

- PARK VMAIL FOR RELAY:

- ESTABLISHING FLDIGI CONNECTIONS (P = Participant. TS = Target Station):
P - Tune radio to TS mode and dial freq. 
TS - QST QST de WX3F WX3F, QST QST de WX3F WX3F K (call periodically, QST=calling all stations).
P - Align cursor to TS signal. The display center frequency will normally be at, or near, 1500. 
P - Once good copy of the Target Station is established, send WX3F de KB3XYZ, I HAVE TRAFFIC FOR RELAY TO N3XL K.
TS - RR KB3XYZ good copy, pls send your message K.
P ? Send message. Terminate the transmission when image xfer is completed.
