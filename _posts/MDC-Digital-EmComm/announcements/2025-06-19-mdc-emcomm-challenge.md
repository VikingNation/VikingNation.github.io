## Purpose

In the July Challenge you will simulate participation in an ARES Mutual Assistance Team (ARESMAT) event and provide Health and Welfare (H&W) status messages from a simulated wild fire disaster zone. In this Challenge participants will gain familiarity with Radiogram and/or ICS-213 Forms and practice Winlink, VarAC, and Fldigi Peer-to-Peer (P2P) messaging.  Since different ARESMAT teams use differing messaging software, it is important to gain familiarity with as many of them as you can. It is only with trial and error and repeated practice that operator proficiency can be maintained. 
Radio Relay International (RRI) Gold (safe and well) texts will be used in Winlink RRI ICS-213 Radiograms, VarAC ICS-213s, and FLMSG Radiograms, in this exercise. You are encouraged to explore RRI reference materials available at https://radiorelay.org/about/.

SCENARIO:
A simulated wild fire is threatening communities from LaPlata to Waldorf, Maryland. Evacuation orders have been issued and local shelters have been activated. First responders from Prince George's and Charles Counties are on scene, the internet is down in the area.  ARESMAT assistance has been requested from surrounding jurisdictions. MDC Section Winlink, VarAC, and Fldigi Target Stations will be on the air providing H&W message relays from citizens in the disaster zone.

## Task 1

, Winlink messaging: You are simulating an ARESMAT operator deployed to a wild fire disaster zone. Under RADIOGRAM & RRI form templates, select the RRI ICS213 Radiogram template and create a H&W status report message from a person in the disaster zone (see attached Radiogram Instructions). Use the Precedence, Place of Origin, Local time, To: and From: address info provided above. Use at least one text selected from Standard RRI GOLD Radiogram texts (see attached). You aren?t limited to the RRI Gold texts, but use at least one of them.  Click on the button to preview for accuracy before submitting. Send your Radiogram to a Winlink P2P Target Station for relay. The RRI Liaison stations listed as tactical addresses in the drop-down at the bottom of the form are not to be used in the Challenge. You will need to send your message to a Target Station. Put the Target Station callsign in Liaison Station box.


## Task 2

, VarAC messaging: You are simulating an ARESMAT operator deployed to a wild fire disaster zone.  In EMCOMM mode, compose a Vmail ICS-213 form containing a H&W status report message from a person in the disaster zone. On the ICS-213 form, use all of the available originator and addressee info provided above, and revise the Subject line, accordingly. Use at least one text selected from Standard RRI GOLD Radiogram texts (see attached). You aren?t limited to the RRI Gold texts, but use at least one of them.  Approve the message with your name and callsign. send Vmail addressed to your Target Station.


## Task 3

, Fldigi messaging: You are simulating an ARESMAT operator deployed to a wild fire disaster zone. Use the RADIOGRAM Form found in FLMSG and create a H&W status report message from a person in the disaster zone. Use Precedence and Place of Origin provided above. The Hx box should be left blank for this exercise. Comp check-box should be left blank for short Radiograms. The ARL MSG button will NOT be used to create this message. Use Joe Schmo in the SIG: box. Put EXERCISE H&W MESSAGE ? RELAY TO N3XL in the OP NOTE boxes. Place message text, at least one selected from Standard RRI GOLD Radiogram texts (see attached) AND addressee contact Info in the TXT box. Click on red ck button when TXT is completed. Establish your connection to a Target Station (see procedures below). When ready, VERIFY OP MODE, and click on AutoSend in the top tabs menu to send your message to a Fldigi Target Station for relay. Wait on frequency for a short message acknowledgment from the Target Station. 

- ESTABLISHING FLDIGI CONNECTIONS (P = Participant. TS = Target Station):
P - Tune radio to TS mode and dial freq. 
TS - QST QST de WX3F WX3F, QST QST de WX3F WX3F K (call periodically, QST=calling all stations).
P - Align cursor to TS signal. The display center frequency will normally be at, or near, 1500. 
P - Once good copy of the Target Station is established, send WX3F de KB3XYZ, I HAVE TRAFFIC FOR RELAY TO N3XL K.
TS - RR KB3XYZ good copy, pls send your message K.
P ? Send message. Terminate the transmission when image xfer is completed.
TS ? Brief acknowledgement, or request to resend, etc.
