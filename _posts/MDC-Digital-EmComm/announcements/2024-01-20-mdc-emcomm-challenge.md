IMAGE CAPTURE PROCEDURES (Snipping Tool): 
1. Type "snip" in Windows 'Search' and select Windows Snipping Tool.
2. Select "New" in upper left.
3. Holding down the left mouse key, use the cursor to capture the image that you choose.
4. Click on "File" and Save As...

IMAGE CAPTURE PROCEDURES (Paint, best resolution): 
1. Copy your photo or downloaded image.
2. Type "paint" in Windows 'Search' and select Paint..
3. Key Ctrl V and paste into Paint
4. File > Save As ...  JPG file.

PARK VMAIL FOR RELAY:
1. Before making a VarAC connection with a Target Station, it is recommended to prepare (SEND VMAIL button) the message for relay. Once prepared, post the VMail to your Outbox. Once you are connected to your Target Station, go to your Outbox, right click, and select "Relay now through connected station". 

2. For short messages that require no preparation ahead of time (or if you have a copy ready to go), you can connect to a Target Station, click on the SEND VMAIL button, and address to N3XL. The system will ask if you want to "Relay now through connected station".


## Planning

Plan ahead and use your ingenuity and imagination to find, download, or create multiple images of automobile crashes, wild-fires, hurricane damage, tornado damage, terror attacks, or other natural disasters. See the ' IMAGE CAPTURE PROCEDURES' below:

File size limitations to achieve reasonable RF transmission times will vary, depending on the quality of your connection, propagation conditions, and equipment limitations. The nominal file size limit for Winlink and VarAC is 100 Kbytes, which is generally too large for RF transmissions. We are suggesting that you resize your images below 50 Kbytes for Vara FM and 20 KBytes for HFor Packet for starters, making adjustments as necessary if the transmission dies or takes too long.

You may use any Winlink mode for this exercise, includingTelnet or any standard RMS Channel. However, if capable you are encouraged to use theTarget Station List and send images using peer-to-peer (P2P) modes to as many stations as possible. The Target Station List will be provided on 6 FEB 2024. Carefully observe Target Station Op Schedules.

Winlink Packet P2P and Vara FM P2P users should consider the use of digipeating to extend their range beyond line-of-sight. A VARA license is required to digipeat..
