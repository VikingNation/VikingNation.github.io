    <p>
      Every June, the Lewis and Clark Trail On The Air event becomes one of my
      favorite operating traditions. I love chasing all the states along the
      historic trail each year. I add stations to HamAlert and turn on notirfication.
      It is great seeing an alert pop-up and you can head to the radio to 
      make the contact and mark another station off the list.

      You can track your progress on the [My progress](https://www.lctota.org/hunter/my_progress) 
      page on their website. Whether conditions are great or challenging, the hunt is always a
      blast and the operators are some of the friendliest you'll meet.
    </p>
