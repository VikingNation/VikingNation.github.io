## Install spotting app ##
<a href="https://hamalert.org/login" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Ham Alert</a> offers a free servie that gives notifications when stations are online.
Head over to HamAlert and create your account and install the app on your phone.
It is great seeing an alert pop-up and you can head to the radio to 
make the contact and mark another station off the list.

## Find an activator
Watch the <a href="https://www.lctota.org/hunter/find_an_activator" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Find an activator</a> page to see when
stations will be online. Be sure to have your antenna and station ready to go to make that contact!


## Checking your progress ##
You can track your progress on the <a href="https://www.lctota.org/hunter/my_progress" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">My progress</a> 
page on their website. Whether conditions are great or challenging, the hunt is always a
blast and the operators are some of the friendliest you'll meet.

I have successfully contated all sixteen states and two bonus stations.
